Best Dresses for Hourglass Body Shape

Discover flattering dress silhouettes, styling tricks, and outfit ideas for hourglass body shapes.

What Is an Hourglass Body Shape?

An hourglass body shape typically has balanced shoulders and hips with a naturally defined waist.

Best Dress Styles for Hourglass Body Shape

Wrap Dresses

Wrap dresses beautifully define the waist and enhance natural proportions.

Bodycon Dresses

Bodycon dresses highlight curves and embrace the natural silhouette of hourglass body shapes.

Fit-and-Flare Dresses

Fit-and-flare silhouettes emphasize the waist while keeping balanced proportions elegant.

Belted Dresses

Belted dresses naturally draw attention to the waist and create a flattering shape.

Styling Tips

  • Choose fitted silhouettes to highlight your waist
  • Experiment with wrap and belted dresses
  • Try V-necks or sweetheart necklines
  • Balance proportions with tailored clothing

Frequently Asked Questions

Can hourglass body shapes wear oversized clothing?

Yes. Many hourglass body shapes enjoy oversized fashion. If desired, adding waist definition can help maintain shape.
